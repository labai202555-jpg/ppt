Title: Al Customer Review Response Generator 

Problem Statement: Hotels and restaurants need to respond to customer reviews professionally and personally across multiple platforms. 

Steps: Use LLM to generate personalized review responses. Create thank you messages and apology templates. Generate follow-up communications and service recovery messages. Build conversational customer service assistant. 

Data Requirements: Review examples, response templates, service recovery procedures, customer communication guidelines in text format. 

Expected Output: Generated personalized review responses and conversational customer service communications.
